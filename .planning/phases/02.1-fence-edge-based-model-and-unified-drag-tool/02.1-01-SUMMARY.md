## Deviations from Plan

**None beyond what the plan explicitly anticipated.**

The plan warned that the `activeFixtureId` reactive subscription in `EditorCanvas.tsx` existed solely to drive `useFenceLineTool`'s dependency list. After removing the hook, I dropped that subscription as well (covered by plan Task 2 step 7: "Remove any local state/refs that existed SOLELY to support the fence line tool"). All other reads of `activeFixtureId` in `EditorCanvas.tsx` go through `useEditorStore.getState()` in event handlers, so losing the subscription has no behavioral impact.
